Welcome to Policy Pulse

Policy Pulse is an AI-powered application designed to simplify the process of generating policy briefs from research papers.

Using a Multimodal Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) model, we extract both text and images to generate concise summaries of key findings and visual data from research papers.
